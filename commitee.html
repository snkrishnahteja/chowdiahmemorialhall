Committee Members

Academy of Music with Sri Mallikarjun Khargeji as its Chief Patron has a 9 member managing committee. Sri Radhakrishna - President, Sri. R. Subbaraj Urs - Secretary, Sri. K. Sri. V. Venkataraman - Treasurer, Dr. K. S. Vaishali - Jt. Secretary.

Members: Dr. Suma Sudhindra, Sri. P. Ramiah, Sri. Gopal B. Hosur, Dr. Indushekar, Sri. W. P. Shivakumar

FROM OBSCURITY TO FAME

Academy of Music has always been at the forefront to recognise and honour legends of the classical arts. It has presented National awards to such distinguished artists by the President and the Vice-President of the country.

Some of the awardees are: Dr. M. L. Vasantha Kumari, Semmangudi Srinivas Iyer, Ustad Amjad Ali Khan, Smt. D. K. Pattammai, Sri. Sheik Chinna Moula, Dr. Padma Subramanyam, Dr. L. Subramanyam, Prof. T. Kunnakudi Vaidyanathan, Dr. Chitti Babu, Dr. Gangu Bai Hangal, Sri. Mandolin U. Srinivas, Sri. K. V. Narayana Swamy, Sri. R. K. Srikantan, Sri. Umalayapuram K. Srivaraman, Dr. N. Ramani, Sri. B. V. Sudha Raghunathan, Dr. Bombay Jayashri, Smt. Ranjani & Gayatri, Dr. Suma Sudhindra and many more.

SCHOLARSHIP

The Academy has instituted “SHARADHA’’ scholarship to encourage upcoming talents and promoting performing arts. Concerted efforts are being made with involvement of a number of dedicated connoisseurs of music, dance and art to achieve the goal within the shortest possible time.
